Overview

Have you ever wondered how the remote works to control a TV? Or how modern appliances and cars operate?

If yes. Then you have come to the right place.

The inspiration from this organization stems from the mind of a creative genius that aspires to become an inventor or creator for the world they interact with in the 21st century.

Experience is not necessary. It can be valuable to see the beginner's mind offer something new to our more experienced members. Everyone at the end of the day is still a student.

Software and Hardware integration are encouraged. Projects Range from building RC-controlled cars to programming machines to running emulations of the most popular retro games made.
